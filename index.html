About This Project

The Hatfield-McCoy Feud Research Database is a comprehensive resource for historical fiction writers, researchers, and anyone interested in this pivotal chapter of American history. This database covers both the original feud period (1863-1891) and its cultural legacy through the mid-20th century (1940s-1950s).

Our mission is to provide historicalally rigorous research materials while making them accessible and useful for creative historical fiction development. All information is verified with proper historical sources and clearly cited.

Research with Confidence

All materials are properly cited with direct links to original sources whenever possible. We clearly distinguish between verified facts and reasonable historical inferences, and explicitly mark gaps in historical knowledge.
