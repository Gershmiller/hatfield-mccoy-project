About This Project

The Hatfield-McCoy Feud Research Database is a comprehensive resource for historical fiction writers, researchers, and anyone interested in this pivotal chapter of American history.

Project Overview

This database covers both the original feud period (1863-1891) and its cultural legacy through the mid-20th century (1940s-1950s). Our mission is to provide historicalally rigorous research materials while making them accessible and useful for creative historical fiction development.

All information is verified with proper historical sources and clearly cited, with a focus on presenting primary sources rather than overly narrativizing history.
